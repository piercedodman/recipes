Crabmeat Appetizers

Description

Ingredients

  • 10 English muffins, split (toasted or untoasted)
  • 1 (6 or 7 oz.) can crabmeat, drained and flaked
  • 1 jar Kraft Old English spread
  • 1/4 lb. butter or margarine
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic poweder
  • 2 Tbsp. mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Spread each muffin half with mixture
  2. Cut into quarters
  3. Freeze on cookie sheets, then put into plastic bags
  4. Keep frozen until ready to use
  5. Heat until golden brown in 425Ëš oven
