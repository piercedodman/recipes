Mushrooms With Shrimp

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. large mushrooms
  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 lb. cooked shrimp, shelled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup bottled Italian dressing
  • 6 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup chopped chives

Instructions

  1. Remove stems from mushrooms
  2. Place caps in bowl, cover with lemon juice, and set aside
  3. Place shrimp in another bowl, add Italian dressing, cover and refrigerate several hours
  4. Mix cheese and chives until smooth and creamy
  5. Drain mushrooms and shrimp
  6. Fill each cap with 1 tablespoon cheese mixture and top with a shrimp
