Shrimp Cocktail

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. shrimp
  • 1 (16 oz.) can tomato juice
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 limes (juice)
  • 1 lemon (lemon)
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tsp. hot pepper sauce
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 2 Tbsp. ketchup
  • 1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook shrimp briefly in boiling water; shell
  2. Place shrimp in shallow pan, add lime and lemon juices; toss
  3. Add all remaining ingredients
  4. Let stand in refrigerator for at least 4 hours
  5. Serve with small pieves of toast
