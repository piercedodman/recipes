Pizza Rustica

Description

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough-bought or homemade
  • 1/2 lb. ham
  • 1/2 lb. large pepperoni, thin sliced
  • 1 lb. sliced provolone cheese
  • 1 (8 oz.) jar pimienos, cut in strips
  • 1 egg mixed with 1 Tbsp. water (for crust)
  • 1 lb. mozzarella cheese, shredded

Instructions

  1. Grease a 13"x9" baking pan
  2. Roll out a piece of dough large enough to cover the bottom of the pan and come over the sides
  3. Layer the ingredients
  4. Fold over the overhangin dough onto the layered ingredients
  5. Roll out a second piece of dough the size of the top of the pan
  6. Place over the layers and folded dough
  7. Pinch edges to seal
  8. Brush top with egg mixture
  9. Bake at 375Ëš for one hour or until golden brown.
  10. Serve hot or cold
