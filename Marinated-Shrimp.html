Marinated Shrimp

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. cooked, cleaned shrimp
  • 3 scallions, chopped (green part, too)
  • 2 dashes Tabasco sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 bottle capers - with juice
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of vinegar
  • 1 onion, finely sliced

Instructions

  1. Mix all together and store in refrigerator 12-24 hours
  2. Drain thoroughly 15 minutes before serving.
